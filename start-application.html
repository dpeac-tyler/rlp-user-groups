Getting Started

Ready to create an online license or permit application for your agency? Here's how to get started:

First, a quick overview:

There are eight (8) tabs across the top of this page. These are the key sections you will complete to create your application. Each section includes step-by-step instructions of what you will need to do. You will also have the chance to review each section and make changes before moving on.

The navigation bar on the left shows where you're at in the process.

When you're done, you will be able to view the entire application as users will see it online.

More information about each section:

1. Application Details - Choose your type of application, give your application a name, add an internal Form Number, provide a brief description, and include a URL where the user can find more information.

2. Applicant/Submitter/Entity Information. Determine the information you need to gather from the user.

3. Question Builder. Choose from different question types to create the questions you need to ask the user.

4. Conditions. Enter logic to show or hide subsequent questions and answers. This section is not required, but it may make your application simpler and easier for users to complete.

5. Payment. Indicate the price of the license or permit in this section.

6. Preview. Take a look and see what you've created and what your user will see.

7. License. Choose effective dates for a license or permit. Additionally, choose a certificate template if you would like a user to receive a certificate as proof of their license or permit.

8. Notification. Create a custom message that users will receive when their license or permit is issued.
